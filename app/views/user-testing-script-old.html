Scenario 1: Updating Transferor Details

{{ govukInsetText({ text: "This scenario involves updating the register for two registered proprietors, Emily Pankhurst and Sophie Green. The tasks will guide you through various actions such as removing a transferor, adding parties, and amending details." }) }}

Scenario Overview

Emily Pankhurst has passed away, and Jane Jenkins is now acting on behalf of Sophie Green. However, there is a mix-up with the instructions, requiring further updates and corrections.

Task 1: Initial Updates

  1. Remove Emily Pankhurst.
  2. Check the details are now correct.
  3. Add a party called 'Jane Jenkins' as a receiver (Insolvency) for Sophie.
  4. Check the details are now correct.

Task 2: Correcting the Mixup

Due to a misunderstanding, Jane Jenkins was incorrectly added. She is acting on behalf of another person on a different title.

  1. Remove 'Jane Jenkins'.
  2. Check the details are correct (Sophie Green has been reinstated).
  3. Add a trustee called 'Sarah Brook' for Sophie Green.
  4. Check the details are now correct.

Task 3: Final Amendment

It was later discovered that the trustee's name is 'Sarah Brooks', not 'Sarah Brook'.

  1. Amend the trustee name to 'Sarah Brooks'.
  2. Check the details are now correct.
  3. End.

Actions Covered

  • 'Remove' (1 of 2 Registered Proprietors)
  • 'Add a party'
  • 'Remove' (a party)
  • 'Add a trustee'
  • 'Amend' (name)
