Confirm you are registering a new lease

The types of new leases you might need to register include:

  • a new lease (new leases must have a term of at least 7 years and one day)
  • a compulsorily-registered lease
  • a reversionary lease
If your application is a deed or lease varying the term or extent of an existing lease, go back and select ‘Registering a lease extension (varying the term or extent of an existing lease or closing an old lease and registering a new lease for the same property to the same tenant)’.
