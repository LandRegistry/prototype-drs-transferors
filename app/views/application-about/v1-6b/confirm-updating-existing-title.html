Confirm you are updating details for an existing title

Use this application if you are updating, adding, removing or noting information related to a title on the register. This might include:

  • charges
  • deed or release of easement
  • deed of release of covenant
  • deed varying a registered title which does not increase the term of a lease
  • noting a lease
  • variations
  • rectifications
  • notices and restrictions
