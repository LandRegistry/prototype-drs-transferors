What application statuses mean

Validating

The information in your application is still being validated. This status will change to Received if there are no errors, or Rejected if we do find errors.

Rejected

Your application will show as Rejected if errors are found and will not be on the day list. You will need to open the application and resolve the errors before you can send it for validation again.

Received

Your application has been successfully validated and is received on the day list.

Draft

You are still working on the application and it has not been sent for validation.
